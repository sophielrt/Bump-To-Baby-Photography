About me

Hi, I’m Sofia – a passionate photographer, storyteller, and proud specialist in capturing life’s most precious moments here at Bump to Baby Photography. My journey into photography began with a love for preserving the beauty and connection found in everyday life. Over the years, I’ve honed my craft to focus on what I love most: creating timeless images of growing families, from the glow of pregnancy to the laughter-filled chaos of family life. I understand how fleeting these moments can be – a baby’s first yawn, the excitement of an expectant parent, or the unique bond shared between siblings. My goal is to freeze these moments in time, offering you beautiful, heartfelt portraits that will bring joy for generations to come. With a relaxed and caring approach, I aim to create a comfortable environment where your family can simply be themselves. Whether we're capturing the tender moments of a newborn session, the radiant joy of a maternity shoot, or the shared love in a family portrait, I’m here to guide you every step of the way. Thank you for trusting me with your most treasured memories. I can’t wait to help tell your family’s story through my lens.

Services

Bump

Celebrate the beauty of your pregnancy with our bump photography sessions. We capture the glow and love of this special time with artistic and heartfelt portraits. Whether you prefer a serene studio setting or an outdoor backdrop, we create images that honor your unique journey into motherhood.

Newborn

Your baby's first days are fleeting, but the memories can last forever. Our newborn photography sessions are designed to capture the innocence, tiny details, and tenderness of your little one in their earliest moments. With a gentle, safe, and relaxed approach, we ensure a stress-free experience for you and your baby.

Baby

Every milestone in your baby’s first year is worth celebrating! From toothless smiles to curious expressions, our baby photography sessions focus on capturing the personality and charm of your growing child. These timeless portraits will be cherished for generations to come.

Family

Family is everything, and our family photography sessions are all about showcasing your bond. Whether it's a casual gathering to celebrate the arrival of the newborn into your family or a styled shoot, we create fun and heartfelt portraits that reflect the love, laughter, and connection within your family.
